FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore how AI Agentic Automation can transform your financial operations.

What is Emblematic and how does it work?

Emblematic leverages proprietary AI technology to fully automate the bookkeeping process, including transaction categorization into management accounts, reconciliations, and IFRS/GAAP compliance. We contextualize each transaction by analyzing historical data, processing all related documents, and applying both business and regulatory logic to ensure accurate categorization in the general ledger.

Who is Emblematic it for?

For accounting teams seeking bookkeeping automation, enhanced accuracy, and maximum data value, our solution is the ideal choice. Our platform offers extensive customization options to suit every company type and accounting structure.

Is Agentic Automation fully autonomous?

We adopt a human-in-the-loop approach, ensuring that all work is thoroughly validated with well-defined approval workflows. Our AI agents execute workflows and propose journal entries, but no changes are made without your accountant 's explicit approval.

Can it be audited?

Yes, our AI agents provide full traceability of their reasoning and the steps taken to execute each journal entry. You can audit and customize every step to align with your desired outcomes, ensuring complete oversight of the entire process. Additionally, we offer comprehensive documentation to support both internal and external audits.

What about data privacy?

We never use your data to train our models. We prioritize your data privacy and security, ensuring that your data remains completely secure and under your control. Our systems are designed to protect your information at every step, maintaining its confidentiality and integrity.
